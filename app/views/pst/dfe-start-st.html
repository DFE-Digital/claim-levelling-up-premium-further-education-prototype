DfE Sign-in

DfE Sign-in is how schools and other education organisations access DfE online services.

To access your DfE online services, you'll either need:

  • an existing DfE Sign-in account. This will be the email address and password you created when you first accessed DfE Sign-in.
  • to create a DfE Sign-in account. If you haven't used DfE Sign-in before you'll need to create a DfE Sign-in account.
By signing in you accept DfE Sign-in terms and conditions.
