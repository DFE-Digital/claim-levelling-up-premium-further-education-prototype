Who counts as a teacher for this payment

You must be currently employed with teaching responsibilities. Teaching responsibilities include teaching timetabled guided learning hours and planning, delivering and assessing learning.

This payment isn't for non-teaching assessors, non-teaching leaders or support staff like assistants or technicians.
