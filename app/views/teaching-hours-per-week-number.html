The amount you are entitled to claim depends on the number of hours you teach

This refers to the direct contact time spent teaching students in lessons.

If you teach:

  • more than 12 hours per week, you are eligible for between £4,000 and £6,000
  • between 2.5 hours and 12 hours per week, you are eligible for between £2,000 and £3,000
  • less than 2.5 hours per week, you will not be eligible for a levelling up premium payment
