Provider prototype

Verify retention payments for further education teachers v4

What are we testing?

How well a provider understands their role in verifying a claim.

What do these journeys cover?

The journey covers how a provider receives and opens an email, signs in to DfE, starts to verify a claim, and what happens when they click on a verified claim, leading them to a page to contact the claim team.
