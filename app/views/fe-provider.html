An eligible further education (FE) provider in England must employ you as a teacher

Eligible FE providers include:

  • statutory FE providers, such as FE colleges, sixth-form colleges or designated institutions
  • 16- to 19-only academies and free schools

If you are unsure whether your FE provider is eligible, you can refer to the full list of eligible FE providers [available Summer ‘24] for more information.

Enter the name of your employer. If you work at an FE provider with multiple campuses, enter the name of the FE provider group.

Use at least three characters.
